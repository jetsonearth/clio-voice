# Meeting Insights - VC Investor Call

## Key Takeaways

### Market Validation
- VC firm previously evaluated WhisperFlow (competitor)  
- Strong belief in voice-first category potential
- Recognition that behavior change will take time but represents major opportunity

### Product Strengths Highlighted
- Better latency than WhisperFlow (1s vs 8-10s for mixed language)
- Strong multilingual support (Chinese/English/Japanese/Korean)
- Code-aware transcription with technical term recognition
- File tagging capability for development tools

### Target Market Validation  
- Developer-first approach endorsed by investor
- Developers are early adopters and sticky users ("consider it religion")
- Geographic focus on Asia (Japan/Korea/Chinese diaspora) is strategic
- VCs as user segment confirmed (use Granola for meeting transcription)

### Feedback & Improvement Areas
- Latency consistency needs work (cold start issues)
- Colloquial vs formal writing style needs more control
- Windows support requested for broader adoption
- Personalization features highly valued

### Business Development Opportunities
- Potential design partnership with VC firm  
- Interest in investment monitoring/evaluation
- Connection to Singapore relocation resources
- Validation of adjacent markets (VCs, professional writers)

## Next Steps
- Fix latency consistency issues
- Implement tone/style controls (already built but hidden)
- Consider Windows port for broader market
- Follow up in 2-4 weeks with product evolution
